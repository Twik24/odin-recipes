Breadnegg

Home

Description

This is basically a combination of fried eggs and toasted bread.

Ingredients

  • A loaf of sliced bread
  • Two eggs
  • spices(maggi,salt)
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Heat up your frying pan with a little bit of oil
  2. Remove however amount of the slices of bread you would want to eat, and place it on the pan
  3. Heat it up until it is a bit burnt on both sides, then remove it
  4. Break your eggs and mix it up with the spices (add in moderate proportion)
  5. Fry your egg with whatever style you want
  6. serve yoyr eggs and put it between the bread.
